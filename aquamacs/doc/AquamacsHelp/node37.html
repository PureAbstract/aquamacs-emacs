Enhanced Carbon Emacs Plugin

Enrico Franconi, developer of the former Enhanced Carbon Emacs distribution that had special support for LaTEX editing via its customizations for AucTEX mode, has revived ECE as an external plugin that can be installed on any Carbon Emacs,including Aquamacs. At this time, the ECE plugin 1.0.1 is available at

http://web.inf.unibz.it/~franconi/enhanced-carbon-emacs/
